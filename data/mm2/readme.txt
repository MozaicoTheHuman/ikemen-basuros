how to modify

1: in "data/mugen.cfg"
    [Options]
     modif = ***.def
      -> path of your screenpack
         (ex: "modif=data/add004_bs0/system.def")

2: in your modif "***.def"
    [Info]
     localcoord = ***,***
      -> match for your screenpack
       (ex: 320,180 -> 240p/default
            640,360 -> 480p
            1280,720-> 720p
            1920/2048,1080 -> 1080p/2k
            3840/4096,2160 -> 2160p/4k)

    [Files]
     fight = ***.def
      -> ensure that your lifebar is optimaized
         (ex: "fight=res/fight_1080p.def")
