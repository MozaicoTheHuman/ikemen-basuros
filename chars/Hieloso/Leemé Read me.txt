---------------------------------------------

Puño congelado: D,DB,B, cualquier patada
-----------------------------------------------
Hieloso se lanza hacia el oponente con un puño de hielo que hace mismo daño que su mazo.

Puño de hielo: D,DB,B, cualquier puño
-------------------------------------------
Hieloso se agacha y con su puño genera un puño de hielo adelante suyo.
Solo se puede hacer en el suelo. Recomendable si el enemigo se esta aproximando

----------------------------------------------
SuperUppercut: D, DF, F, any fist
---------------------------------------------
Icy for some reason it generates this movement that throws the enemy up.
---------------------------------------------

Ice mallet: B, F, any fist
---------------------------------------------
Icy generates an ice mallet in his arm and hits the enemy with great force.
It can only be done on the ground. This one was inspired by the Marvel Comics character, Sand-Man
---------------------------------------------

Frozen fist: D, DB, B, any kick
-----------------------------------------------
Icy lunges at the opponent with an ice fist that deals the same damage as his mallet.
This movement is done both on the ground and in the air.
-----------------------------------------------

Ice fist: D, DB, B, any fist
-------------------------------------------
Icy crouches down and generates a fist of ice in front of him with his fist.
It can only be done on the ground. Recommended if the enemy is approaching
-------------------------------------------
